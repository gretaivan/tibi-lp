Community

We believe in the power of community. Flock Off is a community space for Women and Non-binary people to share their frustration and despair at the senseless violence we continue to see against us as we simply try to walk home at night.

Technology for good

We believe in using technology for good. Love it or hate it, technology is an integral part of our daily lives. Smart phones are becoming more affordable and accessible, and we believe that this provides a unique opportunity to help change how safe we feel on our streets.

Global?

We believe in in embracing globalisation. Our international team come from multiple countries with diverse career backgrounds, but we are united by a shared feeling of despair at seeing the same stories of rape, violence and harassment trending in each of our countries. This is an issue that affects us ALL, and that is why we must work together to solve it.

Equality?

We believe in Co-operation. Just like Flock Off is about ordinary women and non-binary people (you and me), taking the power back into our own hands- it is also for businesses who care about their female and N-B customers. We can’t do this alone. We need us all to come together if we are to make lasting change for our children and our children’s children.
